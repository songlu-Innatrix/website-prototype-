A scalable platform for a changing crop protection market.

Technology platform

From pathogen target to product candidate.

Innatrix’s platform identifies critical molecular targets in pathogens and pests, then designs short peptides or RNAi solutions intended to interfere with those targets while minimizing off-target effects.
